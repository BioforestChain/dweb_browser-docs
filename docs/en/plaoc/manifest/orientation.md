Describes the window orientation. Just a string.

## Valid values

- any

  Any

- natural

  Natural
  
- landscape

  Landscape
  
- portrait

  Portrait

- portrait-primary

  Portrait primary

- portrait-secondary

  Portrait secondary
  
- landscape-primary

  Landscape primary
  
- landscape-secondary

  Landscape secondary
